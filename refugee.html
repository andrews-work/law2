Refugee & Humanitarian

The Community Support Program (CSP) is a program that supports a humanitarian applicant to come to Australia. This visa program requires the support of an Approved Proposing Organisation (APO), who will act as a sponsor.

The APO is responsible for helping the visa applicant settle in Australia, including providing housing, employment assistance, and general support services.

Applicants under the CSP must also meet health, character, and security requirements, and are typically expected to contribute to the costs associated with their resettlement.

This program is ideal for families or communities wanting to directly support individuals in humanitarian need, but it is important to seek legal advice to ensure eligibility and compliance.
